OLD LOOK OF FARLIGHT 84


THE LAMPTON MAP





The early versions of Farlight 84 were quite different from what we see today, offering a nostalgic experience for long-time players. The first maps, like Lampton, had a more straightforward layout, with wide-open spaces and fewer environmental hazards, giving players a raw, open-world feel. Lampton itself was a city-based map, but it featured more narrow streets, less verticality, and fewer hidden spots. Players had to rely heavily on basic combat and positioning in the middle of an intense firefight. The lack of modern updates meant fewer fast-paced mobility features, and this allowed for slower, more strategic battles where players could plan their movements more carefully.


Old Heroes and Abilities


OLD HEORES





Several heroes introduced early on didn't make it to the more recent updates. For example, Warden, who had a protective shield and defense-based abilities, was focused on holding down areas and providing support for teammates. Another was Maverick, whose abilities centered on mobility and quick attacks. While newer heroes are now more flexible and feature more complex kits, older heroes felt more niche and focused on particular playstyles, which encouraged teamwork and tactical planning over raw firepower.


Old Jetpacks


The reason why players play farlight before





The Jetpack was one of the most iconic mobility tools in the early days of Farlight 84. It had a limited fuel supply but offered significant verticality and allowed players to fly over terrain obstacles or escape intense combat situations. Back then, jetpacks were essential for creating height advantages, escaping from tight situations, or surprising enemies from above. The balance of fuel management and high-risk maneuvering made them an exciting feature, especially before the map's verticality started becoming more refined in later updates.


The game’s earlier mechanics placed heavy emphasis on manual control and skill. The old jetpacks weren't as automated as they are now, meaning players had to carefully time their ascents and manage fuel usage strategically, making every flight decision crucial.

Vehicles

syrup truck

Syrup Truck: A bulky transport vehicle used for safely moving teams across the map, offering protection from enemy fire. Its main strength lies in its ability to quickly rush to new locations without engaging in combat.

Mobile turret

Mobile Turret: This vehicle features a mounted turret for suppressing enemies from a distance, making it ideal for defense or holding down key positions. While it isn't fast, it provides substantial firepower during combat.

Blazing infantry

Blazing Infantry: A combat-focused vehicle equipped with a flamethrower, perfect for rushing enemies and engaging in close-quarters combat. Its short-range power makes it ideal for aggressive strategies.

Hoverbike

Hoverbike: A high-mobility vehicle designed for scouting and quickly moving across the map, though it has minimal combat capability. Its speed makes it great for avoiding engagements and reaching new locations swiftly.

Hovercraft

Hovercraft: A versatile vehicle that can travel across both land and water, ideal for crossing terrain and repositioning. It also offers light combat capabilities but is mainly used for transport and exploration.

Air beast

Airbeast: A fast, airborne vehicle with heavy weaponry, perfect for rushing and flanking enemies from above. Its speed and firepower make it a strong choice for aerial combat.

hovercar

Hovercar: A mid-speed transport vehicle that allows players to travel quickly and safely across the map without engaging in combat. It’s ideal for moving from place to place while staying out of sight.

Gunboat

Gunboat: A water-based vehicle equipped with powerful artillery, offering both transport and combat capabilities. It’s perfect for rushing enemies from the water or defending against attackers in aquatic areas.

war-falcon

War Falcon: A fast, maneuverable flying vehicle equipped with heavy weapons, designed for quick strikes and aerial combat. Its speed and firepower make it perfect for dominating the skies and rushing enemy positions.
