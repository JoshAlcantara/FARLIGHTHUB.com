BEAU

tips by sportskeeda

Beau’s Assault Rifle Mod Master passive boosts the magazine capacity by 20%, so he will never be left with no bullets in the middle of a fight.

The Explosion-Proof passive lowers the area of effect damage by 15%, which makes Captain a very stable defender in the heat of battle.
